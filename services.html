Our Consultancy Services

We are crocodile farming experts, we provide comprehensive consultancy services to help clients establish and operate successful commercial crocodile production facilities. With decades of hands-on experience, we guide clients through every stage of the crocodile farming value chain.

Our Crocodile Farming Expertise

  • Site selection and farm design
  • Hatchery management and incubation
  • Feeding and nutrition
  • Health and disease management
  • Farming systems and husbandry
  • Slaughtering and processing
  • Regulatory compliance

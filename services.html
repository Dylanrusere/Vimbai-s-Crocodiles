Our Services

We share and impart the information and the research that we have for the benefit of aspiring local farmers and everyone in the country. We are also available for consultancy services at affordable rates. Thus for any assistance in poultry or crocodile breeding, give us a shout and we will send our A-Team to the rescue.

Chickens

For customers looking to purchase live broiler chickens, Vimbai's Crocodylidae Products, Tanning & Taxidermy and Vimbai's Organic Poultry & Crocodile Meat Supplies has a state-of-the-art climate-controlled chicken house where they raise healthy, humanely-treated birds. Customers can visit the farm and hand-select the broilers they would like to take home. Vimbai`s Organic Poultry`s experienced staff are always on hand to provide guidance on caring for live chickens.

In addition to live birds, Vimbai's Crocodylidae Products, Tanning & Taxidermy and Vimbai's Organic Poultry & Crocodile Meat Supplies also offers a dressed broiler chicken service. Their on-site inspected processing facility allows them to efficiently and safely process the chickens, removing feathers and internal organs. The dressed broilers are then packaged and flash-frozen to lock in freshness. Customers can conveniently order boxes of dressed broiler chickens for pickup or delivery.

Eggs Production

Vimbai`s has built a reputation for excellence, delivering the freshest, most flavorful eggs to discerning customers throughout the region.

Vimbai`s also offers a variety of other egg types, including extra-large brown eggs, organic options, and even specialty duck eggs. Customers can purchase eggs by the dozen or in larger quantities, with convenient delivery and pickup services available.

Pork Meat

Customers can choose from a wide variety of pork cuts and products, including bone-in and boneless chops, tender loins, flavorful ribs, and ground pork for recipes.

Vimbai`s also offers premium specialty items like handcrafted sausages, thick-cut bacon, and slow-smoked ham. All of their products are carefully packaged to ensure maximum freshness and convenience for at-home preparation.

Crocodile Meat

In the realm of crocodile meat, Vimbai's takes pride in sourcing and providing the finest cuts of crocodile meat. With a commitment to ethical and sustainable practices, Vimbai's ensures that the crocodile meat it offers is of the highest quality, free from additives and raised in a natural environment. Whether it's succulent crocodile tail steaks, tender fillets, or exotic sausages, Vimbai's Crocodile Meat Supplies caters to those with a taste for the extraordinary.

Our Consultancy Services

We are crocodile farming experts, we provide comprehensive consultancy services to help clients establish and operate successful commercial crocodile production facilities. With 7 years of hands-on experience, we guide clients through every stage of the crocodile farming value chain.

Our Crocodile Farming Expertise

Site Selection & Farm Design

  • Evaluate potential farm locations based on factors like water sources, climate, and land suitability.
  • Provide architectural designs and layout recommendations for crocodile housing, hatcheries, feeding areas, and other infrastructure.

Hatchery Management

  • Advise on egg collection, incubation, and hatching techniques.
  • Develop protocols for hatchling care and rearing.

Nutrition & Feeding

  • Formulate optimal feed rations tailored to each growth stage.
  • Establish efficient feeding schedules and methods.

Health & Disease Management

  • Implement comprehensive biosecurity protocols.
  • Advise on disease prevention, treatment, and monitoring strategies.

Operations & Husbandry

  • Train farm staff on safe crocodile handling and best practices.
  • Provide guidance on harvesting, processing, and product storage.

Regulatory Compliance

  • Ensure adherence to all relevant local and national regulations.
  • Assist with permits, licenses, and reporting requirements.

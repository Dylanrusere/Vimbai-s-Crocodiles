About Us

We are operating as a farming business that is focused on profitable, progressive and sustainable production of premium quality identity-preserved crocodile meat, crocodile skin and other vast farm products like chickens, ducks, eggs.

We hope to give our customers value for their money by giving them the best nutritional meat found here in the Mashonaland region of Zimbabwe. We prefer a clean lifestyle & we're willing to embrace change.

We also offer consulting services on crocodile farming and we also do tours. We offer a wide variety of products and services. Explore the finest organic products and services with us.
